ABOUT

We are a team of data scientists currently attending Lambda School, an intensive online data science course for a career in applied statistics and predictive modeling. Building real, ready-to-ship products with a team of other students generating real work experience is a key feature of Lambda School. This app is a product of that feature.

Gathering data from Kickstarter we have built and trained a predictive model. This model allows a user to find and predict the fail or success of a Kickstarter campain based on variables the user inputs such as category, currency, goal, country, and duration of days. The prediction can then be used to gauge whether it's worth your time to persue your kickstarter campaign.
